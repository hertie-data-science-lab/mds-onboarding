<div class="dsl-hero" markdown="1">
Welcome to the **Master of Data Science** at the [Hertie School Data Science Lab]({{ site.dsl_org_url }}). We are looking forward to having you join us in Berlin.

This guide gathers everything we recommend you review **before the programme begins** - a chance to refresh the programming and mathematics fundamentals that your first-year courses build on, so you can hit the ground running.
</div>

This preparation package gathers the materials your first-year courses are built on, alongside recommended platforms for reviewing the fundamentals in **programming** and **mathematics**. There is no need to rush, and you are not expected to master everything - the aim is to arrive confident with the basics.

<div class="callout" markdown="1">
**Do I already know enough - and what *is* enough?** A fair question, and the best way to judge is against the courses themselves. The **course syllabi** and the **summer bootcamp** below set out exactly what your first-year classes assume and cover. Skim them, see where you feel solid and where you don't, and focus your preparation there.
</div>
